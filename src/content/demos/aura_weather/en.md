The Weather Mirror is a high-level technical demo for portfolios, designed to impress recruiters and clients by demonstrating skills in APIs, performance (60fps), and artistic sensitivity. The entire screen is an explorable map where weather comes to life through shaders, colors, and particles that react to geography and atmospheric data.
