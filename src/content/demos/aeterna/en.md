Aeterna is an interactive and chronological journey that explores the fundamental milestones of human evolution. Through an immersive visual narrative, this work connects the mysticism of the ancient past, the precision of classical engineering, the awakening of reason, the revolutions of steel, and the quantum leap of the digital age, culminating in the speculative horizons of tomorrow. An experience designed to contemplate the imperishable echo of our own history.
